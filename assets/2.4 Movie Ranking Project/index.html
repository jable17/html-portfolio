The Best Movies According to Jade

Top Three This Year

Pride & Prejudice (2005)

This is a masterpiece of a movie that I watch on repeat. The music, actors, visuals, dialogue, and plot are just spectacular. The hand flexing scene in particular is is scream inducing.

The Queen's Gambit (2020)

This is technically a show, but it's amazing. The character development is spectacular, as well as the sense of suspense in every game.

Pirates of the Caribbean Trilogy (2003-2007)

Another one of my all time favorites. The jokes and fight scenes are great, and the romance plot is the cutest
